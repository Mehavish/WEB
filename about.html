About Us :-

    Varcons offers services related to the development of applications, application performance 
    monitoring and infrastructure hosting. Under applications performance monitoring, it 
    offers services based on applications such as Appdynamics, Splunk, New Relic,
    Solarwinds, and Citratest. Under cloud services, it offers migration, architecture 
    automation, performance monitoring and integration services. The development services 
    are based on technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Jquery, AJAX for 
    front-end applications and J2EE, PHP, Struts, Spring, EJB, RMI, SOA, and 
    Hibernate in the middle-end.
